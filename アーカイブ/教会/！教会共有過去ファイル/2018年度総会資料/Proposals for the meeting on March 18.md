**Proposals for the meeting on March 18, 2018**

**1) About the lunch:**

I think we should have self-service when we have the usual curry

lunches.

First we should pray, soon after the service is over, just like last

Sunday. Then we should put one round table just under the counter. There we should put the curry pot and the rice cooker and plates On

the counter we should put salad, bowls, drinks, cups. spoons and

chopsticks. Then everybody can take their own lunch when they

want to eat. We should help elderly people and new-comers. The

preparation time would be 2-3 minutes. Then we can sit down and

eat more relaxed. Now we waste paper cups because we pour in for

too many. Some don't drink, some have their own drink. Only those

who want to drink can pour their won drink. After eating everybody

should bring their plate to the sink. We could take turns to wash the

dishes. When it is not our turn, we can go back earlier. We could also put a simple piggy bank on the counter for 'lunch

donations'.

**2) About the shop\_**

I hope we can have goods like CLC shop in Ochanomizu or

Kyobunkwan in Ginza. Examples: Cards for birthdays, Christmas,

Easter, funerals etc etc. I think we should have Kawaguchi-san's

cards there, too. Also decoration things for Christmas and Easter,

like the egg decoration we use every year. We can have the same

prices as CLC, but we wouldn't need to go all the way to Tokyo to

buy the things. If we have these kind of things, other Christians

would come and buy, too. We can start by having it open once a

week, on a day when Sensei is at church, so the one in charge

wouldn't need to be alone in the building.

**3) About the rest of the 3rd floor:**

I think we should put it up as renting place. The cost should be per

hour. In the summer and winter, maybe 600 yen per hour and in

spring and fall 500 yen per hour. (That's because of the airconditioning.)

If no one uses it, we would still not lose any money. If

someone wants to rent it, we only need someone to open and close

the place.
