"Let us pray.

Heavenly Father, today we're filled with gratitude as we come before You to

celebrate the birth of this child, スカイラ.

Thank You for the precious gift of her life.

Lord, we ask for Your abundant blessings to be upon スカイラ.

Nourish her every breath and let her grow strong.

As she navigates through life, with its joys and challenges,

may she always keep her eyes fixed on You.

Grant her faith to believe, to pray, to give thanks, and to rejoice in Your presence.

We trust You for her health, for meaningful relationships,

for financial stability, and for a great environment.

We believe You will provide for all of スカイラズ needs.

May her life unfold beautifully, just as You have planned,

allowing her to flourish and bear fruit as only she can,

under Your watchful eye. May she always feel Jesus walking beside her.

We also pray for the parents and family, that You will give them

strength and wisdom to raise スカイラ in Your love.

We surrender all to Your care, Lord. In the powerful and loving name of Jesus, we pray.

Amen."
