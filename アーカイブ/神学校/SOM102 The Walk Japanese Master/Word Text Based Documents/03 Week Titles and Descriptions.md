03 SOM 102 Week Titles and Descriptions

Week 1: The Call of Christ

This unit will investigate the essential role that the call of Christ plays in discipleship. What we will discover is that it is Christ who both calls and qualifies us to be his disciple. Further, we will investigate the unique relationship between discipleship and the cross.

Week 2: The Goal of Discipleship

This unit will explore the telos of discipleship. The purpose is to place us on the correct path leading to the correct destination in our walk with Christ.

Week 3: Understanding Spiritual Formation

This unit will explain and apply the concept of spiritual formation. It will explore the Fruit of the Spirit, as well as the image and identity of Christ in the life of the disciple.

Week 4: Foundational Disciplines

This unit will investigate the two core spiritual disciplines of prayer and Bible reading. What we will discover is that these two disciplines undergird all of the other spiritual disciplines. These two disciplines keep us in communication with God.

Week 5: The Two Boundaries of the Christian Life

This unit will investigate the reality that the Christian life is framed by both a boundary at the center as well as at the fringe of our existence. We will discover that a healthy Christian life is lived in the middle of these two boundaries.

Week 6: Developing Healthy Spiritual Rhythms

This final unit will discuss how we can begin to build healthy and sustainable rhythms in our spiritual life. We will discover that the key to our continued spiritual vitality is the habitual practice of the Christian life.
