(WA) Written Assignment Grading Guide:

20 Response Accuracy: Submission completely and coherently addresses the assignment instructions.

25 Demonstration of Comprehension: Submission demonstrates a thorough and thoughtful comprehension of the course material. Information presented is accurate.

15 Support, Citations and/or References: Includes specific reference details that clearly support observations and conclusions.

25 Organization: Submission is well-organized, including a strong intro, thesis statement that directly connects to body paragraphs, and a thought-provoking conclusion. Content presented is easily understandable to the reader/evaluator.

15 Formatting, Construction & Grammar: Uses a variety of sentence types and precise, descriptive language to establish a formal voice. Contains no grammatical or syntax errors. Submission meets formatting and constructions requirements.

(WK) Template or Worksheet Assignment Grading Guide

20 Response Accuracy: Submission completely and coherently addresses the assignment instructions.

25 Demonstration of Comprehension: Submission demonstrates a thorough and thoughtful comprehension of the course material. Information presented is accurate.

20 Worksheet or Template Accuracy: The provided worksheet or template is utilized correctly. All spaces and/or blanks a filled out accurately and thoroughly.

20 Organization: Submission is well-organized, and content presented is easily understandable to the reader/evaluator.

15 Formatting, Construction & Grammar: Where applicable, text uses a variety of sentence types and precise, descriptive language to establish a formal voice. Contains no grammatical or syntax errors.
